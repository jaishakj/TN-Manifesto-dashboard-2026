# TN 2026 Manifesto Scorecard

Independent analysis of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Election 2026 manifestos. This project evaluates vision, policy depth, specificity, and implementation across 50 dimensions for three major parties: **DMK**, **ADMK**, and **TVK**.

## 📊 Overview

This scorecard ranks manifestos based on concrete timelines, quantified targets, and implementation mechanisms—not partisan intent.

## 🛠 Methodology

Scores reflect specificity and institutional pathways. Each of the 50 categories is scored out of 10 points.

*   **7–8 pts:** Concrete timelines, quantified targets, identifiable implementation mechanism.
*   **5–6 pts:** Vague promise with some specificity, no institutional pathway.
*   **3–4 pts:** Headline pledge, "steps will be taken," no roadmap.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### DMK Strengths: AI & Startups
DMK operates in a different universe regarding tech policy. 
*   **Highlights:** TN AI Mission 2.0, Tamil LLMs, quantum simulators, ₹50,000 cr semiconductor policy, 500 GCCs.
*   **Comparison:** TVK frames startups around "Creative Entrepreneurs" (thin on detail). ADMK only adds CS as a school subject.
*   **Score:** DMK (9) · TVK (6) · ADMK (3)

### TVK Strengths: Youth & Employment
TVK holds a clear edge over ADMK in youth welfare.
*   **Highlights:** ₹4,000/month for jobless graduates, ₹20 lakh interest-free education loans, 5 lakh internships/year, 75% local jobs with GST incentives.
*   **Comparison:** ADMK offers an employment allowance from the 1960s playbook.
*   **Score:** TVK (8) · ADMK (5)

### ADMK Strengths: Direct Cash & Goods
ADMK's sole competitive zone is direct transfers.
*   **Highlights:** ₹10,000 family relief, free refrigerator, 3 LPG cylinders/year, ₹1,000 Pongal cash.
*   **Limitation:** Outside this zone, the manifesto is almost entirely "steps will be taken."
*   **Score:** ADMK ceiling in competitive categories is ~6.
